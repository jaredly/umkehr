I'd like the examples/block-rich-text app to support block nesting. Let's just do `tab` and `shift-tab` at the start of a block to indent / unindent.
If we're unindenting a block that has siblings after it, we'll need to make those siblings be children of the unindenting block. Let's do a similar thing to what we do with split "incidental reparenting", where the `ts` of the `block.order` for those siblings represents the fact that their reparenting was incidental, such that if there are siblings A B C and D, and B gets unindented by one client, and C gets unindented by another, D ends up as the child of C and not B (because B's order.index sorts earlier than C's at the time of reparenting)
