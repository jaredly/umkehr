examples/block-rich-text: Let's make a "kanban/trello" block type! similar in spirit to the table type, but oriented differently. The columns of the board are the direct children of the block (with the column titles being the contents of those children), and children of each "column" making up the cards. Cards that have children would render as "title w/ contents", whereas cards without children could render just as themselves. We'll need careful drag&drop handling, so that a card can be dragged to be rearranged, or moved between columns, or to become the *child* of another card, or moved out of the trello card entirely.
We should place no restrictions on the block types allowed for the cards or rows, although some row block types will render strangely.
