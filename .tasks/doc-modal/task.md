The "document selector" dropdown is trying to do way too much work. For architectures with persistence, it lists both "seed docuemnts" (for which there is no current persisted document) and "real documents", but doesn't make that clear. For the server architecture it's even more complex, as it can list documents that are client-only, server-only, or both, as well as seeds.
Let's make a full-blown modal, that lists out documents with clear indications of what they are, and separately lists non-realized 'seeds' with a button to 'create' or something. We also need the ability to 'delete' documents from local persistence.
